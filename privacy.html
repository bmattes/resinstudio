Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

ResinStudio ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

ResinStudio stores all data locally on your device, including:

  • Mold dimensions and photos you add
  • Supply inventory information
  • Project details and costing data
  • Invoice information (client names, amounts)
  • Business settings (labor rate, overhead percentage)

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Analytics Information

We use PostHog, a privacy-focused analytics platform, to understand how users interact with ResinStudio. We collect anonymous usage data such as:

  • Feature usage (e.g., "calculator used", "project created")
  • App screens viewed
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App crashes and errors

We do NOT collect:

  • Your personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Your mold designs or photos
  • Your business data (project names, client names, costs, prices)
  • Your location
  • Unique device identifiers that could identify you personally

Subscription Information

Subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives:

  • Your Apple ID subscription receipt
  • Subscription status (active, expired, cancelled)
  • Subscription type (monthly, annual)

We do not have access to your payment information. All payment processing is handled by Apple.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the ResinStudio app
  • Verify your subscription status
  • Understand feature usage to improve the app
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Communicate with you about updates or support requests

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: All your molds, supplies, projects, and invoices are stored locally on your device using SQLite. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it (e.g., sharing an invoice PDF).

No Cloud Sync: ResinStudio does not sync your data to any cloud service. If you delete the app or switch devices, your data will not be automatically transferred.

Analytics Security: PostHog analytics data is anonymized and stored securely. We do not share this data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

ResinStudio uses the following third-party services:

RevenueCat

Manages subscription status. See RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

PostHog

Anonymous analytics to improve the app. See PostHog's privacy policy: https://posthog.com/privacy

Apple App Store

Processes all payments and subscriptions. See Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally on your device and accessible within the app.
  • Deletion: Delete the app to remove all locally stored data. To request deletion of analytics data, contact us at bmattes+resinstudio@gmail.com.
  • Export: You can export invoices as PDFs. Other data can be manually exported by taking screenshots or contacting support for assistance.
  • Opt-out: Analytics cannot be disabled in the current version, but all data is anonymous and does not identify you personally.

Children's Privacy

ResinStudio is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Legal basis for processing: We process your data based on consent (analytics) and contractual necessity (subscription management).
  • Data retention: Analytics data is retained for 2 years. Subscription data is retained as long as your subscription is active.
  • Right to access, rectify, or erase: Contact us at bmattes+resinstudio@gmail.com to exercise these rights.
  • Right to lodge a complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

CCPA Compliance (California Users)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to know: We do not sell your personal information. We collect anonymous analytics data as described above.
  • Right to delete: Contact us at bmattes+resinstudio@gmail.com to request deletion of analytics data.
  • Right to opt-out: We do not sell personal information, so there is nothing to opt-out of.
